My name is Katya and I’m based in lovely seaside town Bray in Ireland. Papeire is my long-term dream coming true.

The touch and smell of paper, old photographs, papers, magazines and maps - these all things were in my life since its beginning. My first attempt at crafting happened when I was 4 years old and cut out all the flowers from the book… My parents weren’t too happy about that :)

Many things changed since then. Currently, I’m specialised in creating handmade journals and cutouts and deal with all-kind-all-form vintage paper.

If you’re looking for unique handmade stationery, you are in the right place. Browse all products or contact me if you have any questions - but check first FAQ in case these questions have already been answered.

Happy creating and see you around!
